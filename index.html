Services

Conceptual & Detail Design

Engaged in many design projects at the very early, idea-on-a-napkin stage and carried them through to production drawing generation. Most projects were made in SolidWorks, though I also have experience with CREO Parametric and OnShape. I was reponsible for concept ideation, material selection, COTS part sourcing, vendor contacts, etc.

Prototyping

Developed prototypes of varying complexity: from specific conceptual prototypes to explore a given mechanism, material, or process, to highly-detailed, functional full systems. Made use of 3D printers, laser cutter, OMAX water jet cutter, and manufacturing vendors, as appropriate, for part creation.

Design for Manufacturing

Starting from my 8+ years in the FIRST Robotics Competition community, I have accrued a significant amount of first-hand machining experience. That, coupled with the invaluable discussions and feedback I have received from professional machinists and more senior engineers, has all fed into my design for manufacturing approach. This has given me insight into effective ways to optimize features for machinability, keep part cost down, and streamline production timelines.

Portfolio

Bicycle Light Mount

I bought a new headlight for my bike, and it was great. Then I bought a new bike, and it was also great. Putting the two together - not great. The mount the light comes with (and, seemingly many other lights) can't fit on 31.8mm handlebars. I was devastated. So I spent way too much time designing this modular mount. My light is held securely, but it is still easily removable. The top parts are also removable, so attachments for other lights or other stuff can be designed to work with the bar-clamping parts in the future. Here's to many well-lit rides!
